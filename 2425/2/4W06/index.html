Keigo Higashino

Japan's Master of Mystery

Notable Works & Achievements


  • 1985 - Won the Edogawa Rampo Prize for "After School"

  • 2006 - "The Devotion of Suspect X" won the Naoki Prize

  • 2009 - "Salvation of a Saint" published

  • 2012 - "Journey Under the Midnight Sun" adapted to film

  • 2014 - "The Name of the Game is a Kidnapping" released

  • 2020 - "Silent Parade" published

"Sometimes, the more you think about something, the more impossible it becomes to act."

- Keigo Higashino, The Devotion of Suspect X

Literary Impact


Higashino has revolutionized the mystery genre with his innovative "inverted detective story" style, where the focus is not on "whodunit" but "howdunit" and "whydunit".
