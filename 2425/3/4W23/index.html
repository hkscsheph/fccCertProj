Introduction

Deepwoken is a permadeath game with high stakes.

Players can explore various regions, engage in combat, and build their own characters.

The game features a unique permadeath mechanic, adding a layer of challenge.

Players can choose from different build stats and abilities to customize their gameplay.

Understanding the game mechanics is essential to survival.

Build

A good build is the first step in your Deepwoken journey.

Each race has a different play style.

Exploration

Exploration is a core aspect of Deepwoken.

Players can traverse the sea, from Etris to New Kyrsa.

Each area has unique challenges and rewards.

Be on the lookout for hidden treasures and secrets.

Utilizing your abilities can help you navigate difficult areas.
